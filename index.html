About Me

Who I Am

Hi, I'm Zeynep Göktepe — a Computer Science student and researcher at the California Institute of Technology specializing in intelligent systems, machine learning, and human-computer interaction.

My work bridges the gap between complex algorithmic structures and practical applications—spanning from developing ML pipelines for differentiable neuron modeling to deploying full-stack mobile applications and publishing iOS platforms with thousands of active downloads.

I thrive on creating systems that are mathematically rigorous, highly optimized, and visually intuitive. Whether training diffusion models or integrating multi-threaded autonomous robotics, my focus is pushing the boundaries of what intelligent software can achieve.
